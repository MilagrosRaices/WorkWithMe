about


The library is a magical place, one where time seems to pause and accelerate at the same time. Whether you visited the library to pick up some new books, to dive into a fictional world, or simply to get some work done, the library was there to welcome you.

Although the atmosphere of a real library cannot be replaced, I hope this page can keep you company at home as we wait for the pandemic to finally come to an end, and we can meet again safely. Until then, pick up a book and sit awhile.

IMissMyLibrary includes a to-do list and library sound effects to help you stay comfortable and productive. You can adjust the volume of the sounds to your liking. Chrome is recommended for the best experience.

Inspired by IMissMyCafe, IMissMyBar, and IMissMyOffice. Sound effects obtained from ZapSplat. Code by Ellie Xing and illustration by Sophie Wang. No copyright infringement intended.
